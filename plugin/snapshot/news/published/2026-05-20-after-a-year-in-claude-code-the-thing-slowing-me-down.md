The author shares a year-long experience using Claude Code, discovering that personal habits and attention management, rather than model or prompt improvements, were the main bottlenecks to speed. They categorize common slowdowns into four types—connect, encode, teach, and parallelize—and offer practical fixes, especially emphasizing running multiple agents in parallel to better use attention.
