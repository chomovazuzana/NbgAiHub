`/claudemd` drops the team's standard `CLAUDE.md` template into your current folder and finalises the placeholders (project name, tech stack, ports, etc.) by inspection.

Use it on day one of any new project. The resulting file is what every future Claude session reads on session start to learn your project's conventions — without it, you're paying the cold-start tax every time.

Existing project without a `CLAUDE.md`? You can also just ask Claude to propose one from looking at what's already there.
