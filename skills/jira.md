`jira` lets you use JIRA without opening the JIRA UI. Ask Claude in plain English ("create a ticket for this bug", "what's in my sprint", "move TICKET-123 to In Progress") and the skill handles the API calls.

Use it: any time you'd otherwise context-switch to the JIRA browser tab. Stays in the terminal, stays in the flow.

Triggers: "create JIRA ticket", "search JIRA", "update JIRA issue", "transition issue", "sprint planning", "epic management".
